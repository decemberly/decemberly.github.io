About me

I've introduced designers to modular design, built deploy systems with back-end developers and been lead developer on several projects. What I enjoy the most is making great ideas come to life on screen.

Apart from being a front-end developer I also run a web and communications agency with clients such as Médecins Sans Frontières and Sweden for UNHCR. I have studied graphic design and web development and have several years of experience working as a consultant.

I am able to work effectively in a team or on my own. I enjoy a challenge and do not avoid responsibility.

I want to work on projects that I can be proud of, be a part of amazing teams and develop my skill set. If you're interested in helping me make this happen, let's get in touch.
