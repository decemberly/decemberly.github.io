About me

I've introduced designers to modular design, built deploy systems with back-end developers and developed several sites on my own.

Apart from being a front-end developer I also run a web and communications agency with clients such as Médecins Sans Frontières and Sweden for UNHCR.

I have studied graphic design and web development and have several years of experience working as a consultant with customer contact for an agency.

I want to work on projects that I can be proud of, be a part of amazing teams and develop my skill set.

Accessibility and usability are important aspects of every project,
therefore I believe in a close collaboration between UX, designer and front-end.
